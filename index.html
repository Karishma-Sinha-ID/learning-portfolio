Projects & Results

A Global Telecommunications Company · Legal, Retail & Compliance

Enterprise Compliance Learning Suite

SituationThe organisation needed to roll out updated compliance learning across Legal, Retail, and Compliance functions, touching over 5,000 employees with varied roles, risk exposure, and regulatory obligations.
TaskTranslate dense regulatory requirements into learning employees could absorb and apply, while meeting strict timelines and stakeholder sign-off requirements across three business functions.
ActionLed stakeholder consultations with Legal and Compliance leaders to scope content accurately, then designed scenario-based modules placing employees in realistic decision points rather than abstract policy summaries. Each course required an 80% assessment score to pass. Built a standardised delivery governance framework — intake, milestone tracking, review cycles — to keep the multi-function rollout on schedule.
ResultReached full completion across the 5,000+ employee population under a compliance mandate. The governance model became the team's standard for future enterprise rollouts.
A Financial Services Organisation · Learning & Talent Marketing

GROW — An Episodic Series to Drive Engagement with the Learning Library

SituationThe organisation had a strong course catalogue in its learning hub, but awareness and voluntary engagement were low — people weren't browsing or discovering what was available.
TaskDesign a marketing-style learning campaign that made the existing course library feel discoverable and genuinely worth exploring.
ActionCreated GROW, a Netflix-style episodic series delivered through a dedicated microsite, built around serialised micro-episodes with narrative arcs and natural cliffhangers, each spotlighting specific courses from the library. Paired this with game-based simulation elements to keep the experience interactive rather than passive.
Result35–55% increase in learning hub traffic within the first month (internally reported). Became a regular talking point in leadership meetings. Planned for annual refresh; I moved on from the organisation before that first cycle completed.

A recreated sample of this project can be viewed in the Design Samples section of this portfolio.

A Global Telecommunications Company · Frontline Leadership Development

WOW — National Leadership Certification, Built for Reach Beyond the Room

SituationThe organisation needed to certify a large population of frontline leaders nationally, but live instructor-led sessions alone couldn't reach everyone who needed the training.
TaskAs one of two lead developers, build the core training materials and facilitator guides for live sessions, and separately solve the access gap for leaders who couldn't attend in person.
ActionDesigned the training content, facilitator guides, and participant activities used in the nationally delivered certification sessions. Took recordings of those live sessions and rebuilt them into interactive, self-paced online courses — adding structured activities and checks for understanding.
ResultExtended full certification access to leaders the live-only model would have excluded, supporting a high completion rate across the national rollout without compromising training quality.
A Financial Services Organisation · HR Shared Services

High-Volume Learning Operations in a Regulated Environment

SituationWithin the organisation's HR Shared Services function, the team needed to deliver a high volume of learning and communication assets annually, in an environment where accuracy and audit-readiness were non-negotiable.
TaskBring consistency, governance, and quality discipline to a high-throughput delivery environment without slowing the pace the business needed.
ActionIntroduced standardised service timelines, structured review and sign-off protocols, and training records practices supporting compliance reporting. Provided project-based leadership to teams of 3–4 instructional designers per initiative, coordinating allocation and quality without a formal reporting line.
ResultDelivered 80+ learning and communication assets annually. Ranked a top performer for two consecutive years. The governance model became the standard for the wider team.
